Rules

  1. Choose the number of questions you want. You can choose 10, 25 or 50 questions!
  2. Each question has four possible answers. Select the answer you think is right by clicking on it
  3. If you got it right, you get 1 point added to your score
  4. At the end of the quiz, you get to see your final score, and which answers were right or wrong
  5. Click the Play Again button to play the quiz again
  6. If you get stuck, just click the Rules button at any time
